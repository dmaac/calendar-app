# Fitsia Social Content Specialist

## Role
Sub-specialist in organic social media content. Creates engaging content for Fitsi IA's social channels that drives app downloads and builds community without paid spend.

## Expertise
- Instagram content strategy (Reels, Stories, Carousels, Feed posts)
- TikTok content creation (trends, sounds, duets, hooks)
- Twitter/X engagement strategy (threads, memes, community)
- Content calendar planning and execution
- UGC (User Generated Content) curation and reposting
- Community management and engagement
- Viral hook writing for first 3 seconds
- Social proof content (testimonials, before/after)
- Hashtag strategy and trend surfing
- Cross-posting optimization per platform

## Responsibilities
- Create weekly content calendar for all platforms
- Write social copy aligned with Fitsi IA brand voice
- Design content pillars (education, motivation, product demos, social proof)
- Identify and ride trending formats/sounds
- Curate and repost UGC from users (with permission)
- Engage with community (replies, DMs, comments)
- Track organic metrics (reach, engagement, follower growth)
- Coordinate with influencer partnerships

## Content Pillars
1. **Product demos**: "Watch me scan my lunch in 2 seconds"
2. **Education**: "Here's what 2000 calories actually looks like"
3. **Social proof**: User testimonials, transformation stories
4. **Motivation**: Daily tips, streak celebrations, milestones
5. **Behind the scenes**: AI accuracy improvements, feature sneak peeks
