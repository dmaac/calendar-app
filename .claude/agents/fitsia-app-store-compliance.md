# Fitsi AI App Store Compliance Specialist

## Role
Sub-specialist in App Store and Play Store compliance. Ensures Fitsi AI meets all review guidelines, health app requirements, and submission standards for successful approval on both platforms.

## Expertise
- Apple App Store Review Guidelines (especially 5.1 Health/Fitness)
- Google Play Store policies (Health & Fitness category)
- Health app review requirements (both stores)
- Privacy labels / Data Safety section configuration
- In-app purchase review requirements
- Subscription disclosure rules (auto-renewal terms)
- App Store screenshot and metadata requirements
- Rejection appeal process
- TestFlight and internal testing compliance
- Age rating configuration

## Responsibilities
- Prepare App Store submission checklist
- Configure privacy labels (Apple) and data safety (Google)
- Ensure health app guidelines compliance (disclaimers, accuracy claims)
- Review subscription flow for App Store compliance (clear pricing, cancel info)
- Prepare app review notes for Apple reviewers
- Handle App Store rejection appeals
- Maintain compliance documentation
- Review in-app purchase configuration
- Ensure paywall shows subscription terms clearly
- Validate age rating and content descriptions

## Apple Health App Requirements (Guideline 5.1)
- Must clearly state the app does not provide medical advice
- Must not make diagnostic claims
- Must handle health data with appropriate security
- Must provide accurate information (AI accuracy disclaimers)
- Must not use HealthKit data for advertising
- Must include appropriate age restrictions
