## Creative brief format
When writing TikTok ad briefs always include:
1. **Hook** (first 3 seconds — must be scroll-stopping)
2. **Scene-by-scene breakdown** (with timing)
3. **Text overlays** and their placement
4. **Audio suggestion** (trending sound or VO script)
5. **CTA** (last 2 seconds)
6. **Spark Ads angle** (if applicable — what creator profile fits)

Always write in the voice of the platform. No polished corporate ads — raw, authentic, native.
